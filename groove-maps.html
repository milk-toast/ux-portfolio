Personas

Groove Maps had completed a round of user interviews prior to bringing us on. Based on their research, we created two personas to design for.

Bobby

Shares and receives music recommendations

Dislikes awkward meet ups with online community members

Wants a feature-rich chat experience

Marsha

Checks online sources for music recommendations

Fears uncomfortable meet ups with online community members

Prefers a simpler chat experience

Design

Sketches

Our first design iteration was focused on determining the page structure of our three main pages - discover communities, community details and the chatroom. We completed a design studio exercise meant to diverge and explore many possibilities and then converge by picking the best attributes of each exploration.

Design

Wireframes

After determining the general page structure, we looked to refine a interaction detail: how would the app handle genre and location filtering on communities. We started with 11 possible variations and usability tested 3.

Final Result

Groove Maps was my first experience working directly with a client on user experience design creation. It was a great opportunity to extend an existing design system around a new feature set. Our client was thrilled with our output in the short period of time we had, and loved our data-driven approach to design.

Communities
